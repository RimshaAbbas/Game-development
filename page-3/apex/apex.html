In Apex Legends, up to 60 players are dropped onto a large map and must search for weapons, ammunition, and other supplies while fighting to be the last team or player standing. The game features a variety of unique characters, each with their own special abilities and playstyles, and players can choose which character they want to play as before each match. One of the main features of Apex Legends is the ability to revive fallen teammates. If a player is eliminated, their teammates can pick up their banner and take it to a respawn beacon to bring them back into the game.
